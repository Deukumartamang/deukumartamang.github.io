To make the cat5e you need a cable does not matter how long it is. And you
need to strip the outer PVC plastic. The wire inside the cable is twisted to
each other you need to separate them and straighten. To straighten them it is
the hardest part in the whole to make cat5e, your hand will be numb for couple
of minutes, but you will be fine. After that is how it looks like.


After that you must order your wire on this basis. After you have done that,
now you need a wire cutter to cut the wire to make it small, but it should be
in order as well now you need a RJ45 crimp, boot, and a jack.


Now you have to insert the wire into the RJ45 jack. And check whether it is in correct
order or not because once you have squeezed RJ45jack with the help of crimp you cannot
used that again Now once you have inserted the wire in correct order, now you need a R745 crimp to help
squeezed or compress the connector. After you have done that your connector should be look
like this« And you must do that other way around Aswell so that it looks exactly like the
picture above, but do not forget to the RJ45 boot.For the last part now, you need a cable tester,
and you must insert both end to a cable tester. If your cable shows all green without skipping
any of the light, then itis meant your cable is good to use hooray you have done it
